About

Hello!

My name is Joy Dong, and I’m a fourth year at UC Berkeley pursuing a Computer Science major. Despite living in Sunnyvale, CA and growing up with companies like Apple, Google, and Facebook in my backyard, I didn’t take a single coding class in high school because the competitive culture of computer science intimidated me. Looking back, letting my own self-fear dictate my decision to avoid CS was my biggest regret; now, I approach all new things with an open mind, self-confidence, and willingness to learn!

In my free time, I like to go running (follow me on Strava!), try new foods, and think about what type of dog I should get when I finally get one. I also love stickers, religiously watch the World Cup every four years, and I’m scuba certified.

If you’d like to chat, please don’t hesitate to reach out to me at joydong@berkeley.edu
